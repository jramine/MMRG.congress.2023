Objectives of the Congress

The Faculty of Legal, Economic and Social Sciences of Meknes and UCAD University in Senegal are organizing the first international congress: Management and Marketing Research Gates : MMRG 2023 on November 23 and 24, 2023 at the FSJES faculty headquarters in Meknes.

Positioning

The MMRG 2023 is an international congress on new developments in Management and Marketing designed to "Help doctoral students around the world improve the quality of scientific research".

International participants

Many participants from Morocco, Senegal, Spain and Tunisia will presents their recent research in Management and Marketing.

Participants from international Universities

Many participants from Moulay Ismail University of Meknes, UCAD, UM6P will discuss their research in different areas of Management and Marketing.

International Keynotes speakers

MMRG international keynote speakers are from renowned universities: AUI (Akhawayn University), UIR (International University of Rabat) , UCAD ( Univeristy of Dakar) and Universidade de Santiago de Compostela.

Reseacrh Axes

The international congress in Management and Marketing research gates (MMRG) is composed of two main orientations. Within these orientations, there are multiple categories or research axes. Here's the breakdown:

Orientation 1: New Practices and Trends in Marketing and Management

  • Management and Organizational Performance
  • New developments in Management after the covid-19 crisis
  • Social Responsibility and Organizational Performance.
  • Quality Management and Organizational Performance.

Orientation 2: Management, Marketing, and Organizational Performance

  • Marketing and Organizational Performance
  • Marketing and digital communication within companies
  • Impact of digital technologies on marketing.
  • Metaverse as a value creation engine.
  • Loyalty, Retention, and Digital Transformation.
  • Recent trends towards differential uniformities and the Boomerang
  • Marketing trend or Marketing trend.
  • Impact of Customer Relationship Management (CRM) on customer satisfaction.

Orientation 3: Risk Management Strategies and Organizational Performance

  • New research models in crisis management
  • Innovation in times of crisis; Innovation and risk management
  • New digital practices.
  • Mobile technologies.

Orientation 4: Finance and Organizational Performance

  • New practices in Finance
  • E-Banking
  • Banking Risk Management.
  • Financial Risk Management.
  • Green Finance.

Orientation 5: Macroeconomic Environment and Organizational Performance

  • Macroeconomic issues for companies and organizations
  • Monetary policy and access to financing for companies
  • Exchange rate regime and the opening of companies to international trade.
  • Taxation and organizational performance.
